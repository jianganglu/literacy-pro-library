Get your students reading the right books

  • Maximize reading achievement with books that match students’ targeted reading levels
  • Find the best titles for every student using search filters like age range, Lexile measure, reading skill and more
  • Easily assign ebooks to the whole class or individual students
  • Browse collections of ebooks curated by interest and themes like Mystery, Space, and more
  • Support a culture of accountable independent reading with seamless access to book quizzes in Literacy Pro

Instant Access to Performance Data

 

The teacher dashboard has a summary of reading activity at the class and student level, including:

  • Books assigned
  • Books completed
  • Books students read on their own
  • Total time spent reading
  • Total words read
  • Average time spent on each book
  • Lexile levels

Opportunities for Meaningful Interactions

  • Teachers receive an alert when a student submits a note or audio recording

  • They can reply directly to student notes or recordings

  • Search filters make it easy to find and assign the right books for each student

Strengthen and develop independent reading skills

  • Leveled fiction and nonfiction titles cover a wide range of genres and interests
  • Read-aloud function aids fluency for younger and beginning readers
  • Clickable definitions expand students' content-area vocabulary
  • Audio recorder helps teachers to access oral reading fluency
  • Note Pad feature makes it easy to write down thoughts and questions while reading
  • Integrated comprehension quizzes with a Scholastic Literacy Pro subscription support an accountable independent reading experience

Personalized Dashboard Increases Engagement

They can easily keep track of:

  • Their Lexile measure
  • How many ebooks they've read
  • The total number of words read
  • How many minutes they've spent reading
  • Which ebooks their teacher has assigned to them
  • The ebooks they've picked out to read themselves

Interactive, Customizable Support

  • They can click on unfamiliar words to learn and reinforce important vocabulary

  • Students can record themselves reading out loud and
    share the final version with their teacher to review

  • Students can send notes or questions to their teacher as they’re
    reading and receive an alert when their teacher responds
